Experience

Shaping Experiences, Scaling Design, Driving Impact

Throughout my career, I've embraced every challenge as an opportunity to create impactful experiences — for users, businesses, and the teams I've had the privilege to lead. From running my own design studio in Italy to leading global digital transformations for iconic brands like Nespresso, Richemont, and Mews, my journey has been one of continuous learning, growth, and reinvention.

I've scaled design teams across industries, built design systems that ensure consistency and scalability, and driven business outcomes through user-centered solutions. Along the way, I've learned to balance creativity with strategic thinking, ensuring that design efforts align with both user needs and business goals.

This page captures my journey — from hands-on design work to strategic leadership roles — and highlights how I've helped brands deliver exceptional digital experiences while building high-performing teams that thrive on collaboration, innovation, and purpose.
